Patient message on Approve Editable

Sent automatically through encrypted patient chat when you click Approve in the side panel, after all steps are verified. Use {patient_name} anywhere in the message and it will be replaced with the current patient's name.

Quick comment buttons Editable

Add chip buttons that show up above the "Your comment" textarea in the side panel. Click a chip there and its text drops straight into the comment box — so you don't have to retype the same rationale on every prescription.

Quick hold-reason buttons Editable

Add chip buttons that show up above the "Hold reason" textarea in the side panel. Same idea as the comment chips above, but for the orange "Place on hold" flow.

Email templates Editable

Email macros that show up in the side panel under "Email to Patient". Give each template a title and write the body — line breaks, blank lines and indentation are all preserved when you copy it. Use the token {patient_name} anywhere in the body and it will be replaced with the current patient's name automatically.

Side panel layout Optional

When enabled, the progress bar appears in the side panel and expands to show step cards when clicked. Turn off to hide the progress bar and steps entirely.

🔍 Extension Zoom

Adjust how big the sidepanel UI appears. Useful on high-DPI displays where the panel looks tiny.

EverydayMeds Extension
This extension adds a step-by-step prescription workflow dashboard to rx.everydaymeds.co.uk. It reads the Clinical Review, Consultation, Documents and other tabs to check GLP-1 SOP compliance, and lets you mark each step as done — automatically navigating to the next tab.
